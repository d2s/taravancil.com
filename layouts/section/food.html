What is this?

This is where I keep notes about the food and drink I put into my body.

Why?

I've adhered to several diets in the past five years, and while my methods changed, my motivation for monitoring what I consume has always been the same:

I want to live well for a long time.

It's my hope that by documenting:

  • my motivation for selecting a diet,
  • how my body responds to the diet, and
  • complications, doubts, and hiccups I encounter along the way,

I will better position myself to make choices that support my health goals.
